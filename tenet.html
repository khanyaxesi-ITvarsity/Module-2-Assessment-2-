TENET

Thriller, Action, Drama

150 min | 16 L P V

Time runs out.

In a twilight world of international espionage, an unnamed operative, known as The Protagonist, is recruited by a mysterious organisation called Tenet to participate in a mission that unfolds beyond real time. The mission: to prevent Andrei Stor, a renegade Russian oligarch with precognition abilities, from starting World War III. The Protagonist will soon master the art of “time inversion” as a way of countering the threat that is to come.
